UWU DEFT CODERS V5.0

We are hoping to give to chances to several teams for each university to participate in that competition. There will be about 250 participants. It will be 12 hours coding session, intended to provide students with an opportunity to demonstrate and improve their coding and problem solving skill. 

All participants will be provided with refreshments throughout the event and other necessary facilities. We are hoping to grant participation certificates for all participants and cash awards for the winners.
